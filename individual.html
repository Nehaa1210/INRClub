Conveniently set in Edinburgh, YOTEL Edinburgh provides air-conditioned rooms, a fitness centre, free WiFi and a bar. At the hotel, each room includes a wardrobe. Featuring a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries, certain rooms at YOTEL Edinburgh also offer a city view. All units at the accommodation are equipped with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a safety deposit box.
